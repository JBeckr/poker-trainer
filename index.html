Keys shown on each button · Space next hand · 📖 Ranges to browse any chart. Two profiles: GM = The Grinder's Manual (Figs 5–16, 28–36, 53, 57); PB3 = BluffTheSpot Preflop Bible Vol. 3, default. Every PB3 chart (Open, vs Open, vs 3-Bet, 5-Bet, Blind Def) was pixel-scanned hand-by-hand from the book and validated against its stated combo counts. In the 📖 viewer they are drawn exactly as printed: each cell keeps the book's frequency split and its original legend colours (note the palette means different actions per chapter). The yellow “borderline” bands are intentionally left colourless — mixed-frequency cells accept either action; Blind Def additionally removes hands whose cell is <50% a real action. ISO, Sizing, Stack Depth & the postflop drills (C-Bet, Flop Defense, Turn Barrel, River, Value Bet, 3-Bet Pot) use simplified GM-style heuristic models — only clear-cut spots are shown. River is an original extension of Ch 12's barrelling idea to a third street, not a book chart. Flop Defense/Turn Barrel/River/Value Bet difficulty unlocks as your accuracy rises. Auto-advance delay is adjustable (⏱️ bar); the 📊 Dashboard shows your accuracy over time, an accuracy-by-hand heatmap, per-mode breakdown, leaks and tendencies, and can back up your progress as a file. Installed as an app? 🔄 Updates shows when a new version is ready and lets you switch between one-click and fully automatic updates. Ranges for study, not gospel.
